Social Responsibility

What we do

We fulfill our social obligations

As a responsible cold roll forming machine company, Sunway acknowledges the roll forming machine supplier’s role and duty to improve and create sustainable solutions for the community’s most pressing needs related to roll forming machine equipments, employees and environment.

Sunway are committed to empower people and business to own better roll forming machines, we do our best to create shared value and develop a sustainable society for future generations. Such a development path is in line with our values.

Responsibility for employees

We protect the physical and mental health of our employees

As a company that produces and delivers terminal product, we want to provide more convenience to our clients and end users of our products, we also want our employees to feel happy during the production process. Sunway believes that if our employees are in a happy mood to produce and work, this happy mood can be passed on to our customers through our products.

To this end, Sunway provides its employees with a comfortable working environment, adequate rest periods, and safe and harmless production materials. At the same time, we hold frequent employee events to make employees feel happy and at the same time enhance their sense of belonging and identification with Sunway.

As an employer, we can do more than simply provide jobs, but also actively seek for the happiness and development of our employees.

Responsibility for client

We Provide the best quality

During the production process, we actively work towards creating a better future in accordance with the environment and nature. We have always provided society with safe and quality cold roll forming machines, and of course, Sunway does not stop there. As our sense of social responsibility increases, we will always effort to improve the efficiency and quality of forming.

Sunway will continue to conduct research on cold roll forming machine and industries, seek sustainable development, and initiate and implement projects involving all levels of society.

Responsibility for environment

We value the environment

Sunway incorporates product safety, quality and environmental protection throughout the product lifecycle, from the design phase to product delivery. We have a clear idea that we want to reduce the environmental impact of our production.

Sunway actively reduce the waste generated during the manufacturing process. For the wastes from the manufacturing process, we want them to be used for other projects by recycling them. For defective products from the manufacturing process, we prefer to repair them rather than simply discard them. We guarantee that these products will be executed to the same standards as other products and will not cause any inconvenience when used.

Our vission

Give you a better Roll Forming Machine

We manufacture according to methods that value the environment and nature, and contribute to a future that is better than today. With our awareness of social responsibility, we continue our tradition of contributing to the improvement of efficiency in modern manufacturing and to the production of better cold roll forming machines.

Sunway wants to change the quality of life and work of its employees and customers with its social responsibility and corporate mission. The company researches related industries such as window and door manufacturing, roofing manufacturing and solar mounting bracket manufacturing on a sustainable social level, and continues to meet the requirements and expectations of various industries while improving our core business.
